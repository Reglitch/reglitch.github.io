“Refocusing Each Spec Around Their Core Concepts”

Apr 16, 2016
legion, alpha, items, mistweaver, design

I was originally going to give my opinion on each change, change isn't necessarily bad, I feel the weight of removals in comparison to additions speaks volumes without injecting my own opinion into things. For that reason, this post will only list changes.

What Mistweaver Has Lost

  • Eminence, a method of doing damage and healing at the same time. Along with the position-based gameplay of statue and player placement.
  • Crane Stance, along with accompanying buffs such as Tiger Strikes and Vital Mists.
  • Mana Tea, removing a significant amount of proactive gameplay.
  • Chi, killing any remnants of proactive gameplay.
  • Zen Meditation, a method of "soaking" exceptionally hard hitting abilities in raids.
  • The ability to "soak" magic damage with Diffuse Magic.
  • Fortifying Brew, a standard baseline defensive cooldown.
  • Gift of the Serpent.
  • Vital Mists and Tiger Power, two powerful melee buffs to track.
  • Spinning Crane Kick.
  • Uplift, and the interaction it had with Renewing Mist.
  • Jade Mists.
  • Detonate Chi.
  • Touch of Death.
  • Spear Hand Strike.
  • Nimble Brew.
  • Expel Harm.

What Mistweaver Has Gained

  • Gust of Mist.
  • Vivify.
  • Essence Font.
  • Sheilun's Gift.

What you’re seeing in Legion falls under one of two different goals:

-Refocusing each spec around their core concepts

-Reduced reliance on cooldowns, both offensive and defensive
